About

About Out Of Home

Out-of-Home advertising has the ability to reach a broader audience, over an extended duration, reinforcing the relevance of the brand being promoted. Frequent exposure to OOH advertising can enhance brand recall. As consumers encounter the ads regularly, they are more likely to remember the brand when making purchase decisions.

Out Of Home Inventory and Locations

At Sishuba Media, we provide clients with expert analysis to identify optimal locations for their products or services. We can acquire sites in locations such as high-end shopping centers, malls, lounges, and restaurants on and around highways. We also provide clients with various out-of-home solutions by factoring in the assessment of traffic patterns, visibility, and maximum reach to the targeted audience. By leveraging market insights, we ensure that clients secure high-impact sites that maximize exposure and advertising effectiveness, ultimately driving better returns on their investment.

Research and Data Analysis

Our main objective is to gather accurate data about the urban and township market, which is currently our key focus areas. As we grow, we aim to expand our insight and deepen our scope of research through collaborative partnerships with data suppliers. Collaborating with other suppliers will ensure that our data always meets the highest standard of accuracy and relevance.

Our Services

Billboards

Billboards enhance brand awareness by showcasing a business to a vast audience. Their strategic placement in high-traffic areas allows them to attract people of all ages, providing round-the-clock visibility. This continuous presence ensures constant exposure with clear and concise messaging.

Wall Murals

Wall murals serve as powerful conversation starters and effective advertising mediums. Their visibility ensures they capture the attention of a diverse audience and are cost effective. Additionally, they empower communities by providing rental income to homeowners, contributing to their financial stability also giving local artists an opportunity to show case their skills by painting campaign artwork.

Activations

Brand activations create emotional connections between brands and consumers, making them feel more relatable. By immersing consumers in meaningful experiences, these activations drive engagement and encourage long-term loyalty, ultimately aiming to attract and motivate consumers throughout their buying journey.
