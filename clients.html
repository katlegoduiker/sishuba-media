Smirnoff

Smirnoff targeted gentlemen, known as "our Bab Mandla," in township taverns who have been loyal to the brand for a long time. The brief was about getting those loyal clients to go back, buy, and spend time enjoying their drinks at the taverns. The briefers got warm murals in and around taverns to cater to and raise brand awareness for the target market.
